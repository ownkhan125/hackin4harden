2026 Hackin' For Harden

June 6, 2026

2026 Registration Now Open!

Welcome to the 10th Annual Hackin' for Harden memorial golf tournament in honor of Joshua Cole Harden. In partnership with The Legacy Golf Course and The First Tee of Phoenix, we are thrilled to announce that registration for this years tournament, is now OPEN.

Each year this event is a great opportunity to honor and celebrate Josh, while simultaneously giving back to an organization and sport that was his passion. Since 2015, with generous participation from golfers and supporters like you, the year over year donations to the Joshua Cole Harden Scholarship Fund have steadily grown, bringing meaningful purpose and a lasting legacy in honor of Josh.

On behalf of Josh, the Harden family, The First Tee of Phoenix and The Legacy Golf Club, it is our honor to welcome each of you to the 2025 10th Annual Hackin' for Harden.

EVENT DETAILS:

Saturday June 7th 7:30 am shotgun

Four person scramble

$150 per player

18 holes with cart, Breakfast, BBQ lunch buffet, Prizes, hole in one contest, Proximity Prizes
